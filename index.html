Explore East Crest -

Pranavas East Crest is exclusively designed keeping every age person in mind. Property is equipped with Kids play area, Cricket Pitch, Indoor Game area, Skating rink, Badminton court, Half Basketball court.

Amphitheaters, secure entry points, and striking feature walls enhance the community's aesthetic appeal. A dedicated senior citizen area fosters intergenerational harmony.
